Why EVs May Be More Harmful than Hybrid ICE Vehicles

Published on: October 10, 2024

Electric vehicles (EVs) are often viewed as the future of transportation, but concerns are emerging about their environmental impact. The production of lithium-ion batteries, which power EVs, involves significant mining and energy use, leading to a substantial carbon footprint. Hybrid Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicles, which combine electric motors with traditional engines, may present a better solution for reducing emissions while avoiding some of the pitfalls associated with battery production. This article delves into the pros and cons of both technologies.
